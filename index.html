Reflection

The blogging aspect of DBC Phase 0 has been beneficial. It requires us to deep dive into technical aspects we may not be familiar with. It also forces us to reflect on ourselves and question everything we are doing through Phase 0. Unfortunately, I don’t feel I have gotten the most out of the blogs as I would consistently end up doing them at 2am the night they were due. This was due to them being one of the easier aspects of the week and me typically being swamped from already working 60 hours a week. Other than me not making the most of the blogs I do think it’s a great way to see how we’ve grown so much in a few short weeks.





Good question!!

Generally I feel my questions are concise and lead to quick answers. I try to provide full details on every pertinent issue to the question, ask for as little as possible from the person answering the question and say thanks a million times. That said, I did post one question on stack overflow recently that did not get a response. I posted the question with a lot of detail, code to review and a clear question at the start so people knew what they were getting into. So far all I have gotten is one down vote… I also attempted to go into DBC on the Friday before this cohort started and was shunned ☹
